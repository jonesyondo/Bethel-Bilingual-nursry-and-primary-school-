About Bethel Billigual Nursery and primary Accedemy complex

Located in the heart of Nkozoa, Bethel Billigual Nursery and primary Accedemy complex stands as a beacon of educational excellence in primary education. Our school is built on the foundation of academic rigor, character development, and practical learning.

We pride ourselves on following the government curriculum while incorporating innovative teaching methods that make learning engaging and effective. Our distinctive black and pink school uniforms represent our commitment to discipline and creativity.

At Academic Complex, we believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also the overall development of each child through hands-on projects, certified programs, and a supportive learning environment.
